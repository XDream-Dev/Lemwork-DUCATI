The Philosophy

Why This Build

The Rosso Ducati represents the purest expression of Ducati's racing DNA. Born on the track and refined for the road, this Panigale V2 carries the iconic red livery that has dominated World Superbike championships. Every component is engineered for maximum performance — from the Superquadro engine's 155 horsepower to the Brembo braking system that provides unmatched stopping power. The monocoque aluminum frame delivers razor-sharp handling, while the full electronics suite ensures you harness every ounce of power with confidence.

Superquadro Engine — 955cc V-twin with 155 HP for explosive acceleration
Racing Electronics — 6-axis IMU with cornering ABS, traction & wheelie control
Track-Ready Chassis — Monocoque frame with fully adjustable Showa suspension
Iconic Livery — Authentic Ducati Corse red with racing heritage
